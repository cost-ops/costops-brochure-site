Zero trust · Zero agents

Minimum permissions. Maximum visibility.

No agents installed on your instances. No broad admin access. We use a scoped IAM role with read-only permissions that you control. Short-lived credentials, external ID verification, and full audit logging of every scan.

Security model

  • Customer-controlled IAM role
  • External ID for role assumption
  • Short-lived STS credentials only
  • Zero write permissions — read-only scanning
  • Zero agents — nothing installed in your account
  • End-to-end SSL between all services
  • Keycloak SSO with JWT authentication
  • Full audit trail of every action

Simple pricing

We only win when you save.

Cost-OPS charges a small percentage of your total AWS bill — not your savings. This aligns our incentives: the more waste we eliminate, the more value you get, and our fee stays tied to the bill we're shrinking. No upfront costs, no per-seat licensing, no surprise charges.

Every recommendation is backed by real numbers. We track what you implement, measure the verified monthly impact, and report on savings realization over time. If we don't find savings that exceed our fee, you don't pay.
